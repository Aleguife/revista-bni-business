The BNI Recognition Event brings together the members who have stood out the most throughout the cycle — in each chapter and across the entire region

By Fernanda Sodré

BNI Recognition is a special moment dedicated to celebrating the achievements, commitment and contributions of the members who strengthen our business network every day. More than numbers or results, this gathering represents the recognition of the attitudes that build the true spirit of BNI: collaboration, trust and collective growth.

Throughout the cycle, every referral, every partnership and every connection generated contributed to strengthening not just companies, but also professional relationships built on credibility and purpose. This is the moment to honour stories, highlight results and recognise those who make the Givers Gain® philosophy a daily practice.

A night to celebrate achievements, strengthen connections and continue building, together, an environment where entrepreneurs grow by supporting one another.

REGIONAL SPOTLIGHT

BNI OESP I

The best of the entire region

Behind a region with 10 active groups, in the heart of Alphaville, there has been consistent leadership for 14 years: Alfredo Tanimoto. With strategic vision and genuine dedication to BNI culture, he has built one of the most solid business ecosystems in the network — and it is within this environment that recognitions carry even greater significance.

Among all chapters in the region, three names stood out absolutely in the cycle: Vitor Tedeschi, recognised as the best in OPNFs; Fernanda Sodré, awarded in the Visitors category; and Edson Tadeu, highlighted for Referrals. Each, in their own way, embodies what BNI represents — commitment, consistency and generosity in building a genuine network.
