Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

BNI Business Magazine respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data, in compliance with the Brazilian General Data Protection Law (LGPD — Law No. 13,709/2018).

Who We Are

BNI Business Magazine is a digital editorial publication dedicated to connecting entrepreneurs and generating results through quality content on business, networking, and professional development.

Data We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Browsing data: pages visited, reading time, device used — collected anonymously via Google Analytics
  • Email: when you voluntarily subscribe to our newsletter
  • Contact data: when you send us messages or request information

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

  • Send our newsletter (only with your consent)
  • Improve content and browsing experience
  • Respond to your messages and requests
  • Comply with legal obligations

Legal Basis

Data processing is based on:

  • Consent: for newsletter and communications
  • Legitimate interest: for browsing analysis and site improvement
  • Legal obligation: when required by law

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it only with essential service providers (such as Google Analytics), always within the limits established by the LGPD.

Your Rights

As a data subject, you have the right to:

  • Confirm the existence of data processing
  • Access your data
  • Correct incomplete or outdated data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke consent at any time

Data Retention

We retain your data for the time necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy or as required by law.

Contact and DPO

To exercise your rights or clarify privacy questions: privacidade@bnibusiness.com.br
