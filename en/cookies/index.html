Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us provide a personalized experience and continuously improve BNI Business Magazine.

How We Use Cookies

BNI Business Magazine uses cookies for the following purposes:

Essential Cookies

Necessary for the basic functioning of the site. They cannot be disabled, as they are fundamental for you to navigate and use the available features.

Performance Cookies

We use Google Analytics to collect information about how visitors use the site — which pages are most accessed, where readers come from, and how they navigate the content. All information is collected anonymously.

Functionality Cookies

These allow the site to remember your preferences, such as selected language, to provide a more personalized experience.

How to Manage Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in your browser settings. Disabling some cookies may affect the functionality of the site. For more information, consult your browser documentation:

  • Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
  • Mozilla Firefox: Preferences → Privacy & Security
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy

Third-Party Cookies

We use third-party services that may install their own cookies:

  • Google Analytics — traffic analysis and browsing behavior
  • Google Fonts — typography loading
