BNI Summit 2026 MOVEMENT:
where connections become
results

From May 20 to 23, São Paulo becomes the epicenter of Brazil's most powerful strategic networking

By Fernanda Sodré

There is a kind of energy that only happens when you gather 800 entrepreneurs in the same space, all driven by the same philosophy: "Givers Gain" — give to receive. It is not just an event. It is a movement. And in May 2026, this movement arrives in the business capital of Latin America.

The BNI Summit 2026, which will take place from May 20 to 23 at Villa Blue Tree in São Paulo, is not for those who only want to attend lectures and go home. It is for those who understand that real growth happens in the friction between brilliant minds, in the genuine exchange of experiences, and in that handshake that becomes a long-term partnership.

The first Summit: a new era for BNI Brazil

After the success of the BNI Brazil Conferences in Foz do Iguaçu (2023), Itu (2024) and Florianópolis (2025), 2026 brings a novelty: the country's first BNI Summit. More than an evolution, it is a statement that the strategic networking movement in Brazil is entering a new era — in the city that never stops, where business happens at the speed of ambition.

MOVEMENT: more than a theme, a statement

The theme chosen for 2026 defines who grows and who stagnates. Result-driven entrepreneurs don't sit around waiting for opportunities — they move toward them.

The Summit's format is designed to maximize results: lectures with major names in entrepreneurship, practical workshops and structured moments of strategic networking, where connections are intentional, qualified, with real business potential.

800 entrepreneurs. All BNI members. All committed to the philosophy of adding value before asking. All understanding that referring is not a favor — it is an investment in relationships that generate sustainable results.

Imagine four days immersed in this ecosystem. Waking up and exchanging ideas with entrepreneurs from other states. Joining workshops and leaving with concrete action plans. Closing the day in conversations that turn into partnerships that reshape your next quarter.

The business capital as catalyst

Hosting the event in São Paulo is not just logistics — it is strategy. The city concentrates the largest number of business opportunities in Latin America. It is where decisions that impact the entire country are made.

Villa Blue Tree offers the perfect infrastructure: spacious networking areas, workshop-ready rooms, and spaces for those deeper conversations that happen outside the official program.

For those ready to grow

The BNI Summit 2026 is for entrepreneurs who have already understood that isolated growth has a ceiling — and that network growth is exponential. It is for those who want to maximize their visibility, build solid partnerships and deliver consistent results.

If you are a BNI member committed to real action — not just intentions — this is your event.

The movement begins now

From May 20 to 23, 2026, more than 800 leaders will be in São Paulo. But the real value is not only in the official program. It is in the conversations during breaks. In the partnerships born over coffee. In the contact you make on day one that, six months later, becomes your biggest client of the year.

That is movement. That is BNI Summit.

The question is not whether you can be there. The question is: can you afford not to be?
