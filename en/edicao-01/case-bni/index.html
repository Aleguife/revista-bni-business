When a group bets on a dream:
the story of Anderson "Nen" and the true
meaning of "Givers Gain"

In December 2024, BNI Alphaville proved that networking goes far beyond referrals — it is about believing in the potential of those who have not yet had the chance to shine

By Fernanda Sodré

There is a moment in every entrepreneur's life that defines everything: when someone believes in you before you even have proof that it is going to work. For Anderson Nen, creator of @camera.nen, that moment came through the hands of 35 people who barely knew him, but who saw in him something he himself was perhaps still discovering.

The meeting that changed everything

Elisa de Lima met Nen when he was still just another young videomaker trying to make his way in the market. They did a few one-off jobs together. Nothing extraordinary. Just the kind of projects that happen when you are starting out — small, but done with a dedication that catches the eye.

And that was exactly what the leadership trio (Vitor, Elisa and Philip) noticed: the dedication. The care. The effort of someone who was not just doing a job, but building a name, a reputation, a future.

"He won over the BNI members", says Elisa. Not with big portfolios or million-dollar cases. But with something much rarer in the business world: authenticity and a genuine desire to deliver the best.

The problem many face, but few admit

Nen had everything he needed to grow. Talent, dedication, vision. He was only missing one thing: access. Access to the right rooms. To the right people. To the opportunities that turn potential into results.

When the chance to join BNI Alphaville came up, the answer should have been an immediate "yes". But the financial reality spoke louder. The monthly fee, as much as it represented an investment in the future, was beyond the reach of someone who was still building the foundations of his business.

For many, this would be the end of the story. One more talent who did not get the chance. One more "what if..." that never became reality.

But BNI Alphaville had other plans.

When 35 people decide to change the game

What happened next was not calculated. It was not planned in strategic meetings or debated in committees. It was simply... human.

Thirty-five members of BNI Alphaville got together and pooled the money. Not because they expected an immediate return. Not because Nen already had an impressive client list to offer referrals from. They saw his potential. And at BNI, when you see potential, you invest.

It was immediate. There was no hesitation. There was no "let's think about it". It was a collective movement guided by something that defines the essence of "Givers Gain": give first, without calculating the return.

The trio affectionately became his sponsors. And in December 2024, he joined the group that bet on him before he himself was sure he could make it.

"BNI means the connection that elevates your professional and personal potential. It put me in rooms I would not have had access to and gave me the chance to be with people I would never have talked to. It raised the quality of my service and the frequency of my clients." reflects Nen

But the numbers tell only part of the story. The visible part of the transformation.

The invisible part is even more impressive: Nen left his salaried job. That safety net everyone tells you to never give up. That guaranteed paycheck at the end of the month that pays the bills, but also limits dreams.

He traded security for possibility. And possibility became reality.

Today, Nen no longer works alone. He already has people working with him. From freelance videomaker to entrepreneur with a team. From someone who received opportunities to someone who creates opportunities for others.

And the best part? "He is already setting aside the money to pay for his own renewal", says Elisa with the pride of someone who has watched a son take his first steps.

What this story really means

It is easy to look at this story and think: "How nice, a happy ending". But it represents something much bigger than an individual success case.

It represents what happens when a network understands that its value lies not just in the referrals that circulate, but in the lives it transforms. When 35 successful entrepreneurs reach into their own pockets to give someone who is just starting out a chance, they are not doing charity — they are investing in strengthening the entire network.

Because today, Nen is not just another member. He is a member who understands, viscerally, the power of a network that believes. And that understanding turns into loyalty, into commitment, into a genuine desire to give back to the next person who arrives.

It is "Givers Gain" in its purest form: giving without expecting to receive from the same person, but knowing that when you strengthen the network, the network strengthens you back.

Elisa did not just introduce Nen to BNI. She walked alongside him. She watched the transformation up close. She celebrated every achievement. And today, she watches with satisfaction something that every good mentor wishes for: to see her protégé not only survive, but thrive.

"He left his salaried job and today he already has people working with him", she says. And in that simple sentence the whole journey is contained: the courage to bet on yourself, the ability to grow fast when you are in the right environment, the maturity to turn opportunity into result.

The invitation that remains for all of us

Nen's story is not an exception. It is an example. An example of what can happen when a network decides to be more than a business group — decides to be a community that transforms lives.

How many extraordinary talents are out there, just waiting for an opportunity? How many "Nens" are one step away from transforming not only their own lives, but strengthening an entire network?

BNI Alphaville showed that when you invest in people, you are not spending — you are multiplying. Multiplying talents, opportunities, success stories and, most importantly, multiplying the true meaning of doing business with purpose.

Today, when Nen walks into a BNI meeting, he is no longer the kid who needed a hand. He is the entrepreneur building a solid business. He is the member already planning his renewal. He is the living proof that when 35 people believe in you, you cannot help but believe in yourself.

Because that is how the cycle is completed. That is how networks become movements. That is how "Givers Gain" stops being just a phrase and becomes a way of life.
