The physiotherapist who searched for 26 years — and finally found

By Fernanda Sodré - Photo: Fayos Alves

Andrea had a problem. Not with herself — with the physiotherapy she practiced.

26 years of clinical experience. Thousands of consultations. Good results and others excellent. But patients kept coming back. They always came back.

She treated the knee, the pain migrated to the hip. She treated the lower back, the problem appeared in the shoulder. Isolated exercises. Temporary results.

"There has to be a better way," she thought every time she saw a 'cured' patient return months later.

The encounter that changed everything

Andrea experienced distinctive body practices and discovered Instituto Trata.

"My vision for a more integrated rehabilitation has always been my guideline, from my own experience. But what fascinated me about Instituto Trata was combining technology in complementary assessment with conventional physiotherapy," Andrea recounts.

The differential was clear: an increasingly precise assessment makes the treatment more targeted, more effective in the rehabilitation process.

And the TrataScan did exactly that — it detected compensations invisible to the naked eye, mapping the body in motion in detail.

"It was like finally finding the method I had always looked for." The decision was immediate: Andrea became a franchisee.

When the body moves again as it should

The opening of Instituto Trata in Aldeia da Serra brought three differentials that revolutionize treatment:

Integrated vision of the body

Knee pain? The assessment includes a much broader view than specific tests of the knee alone. Because the body works as a system. When one part compensates, another suffers.

"You don't have 'a bad knee'. You have a movement pattern overloading the knee," Andrea explains.

TrataScan: technological precision

The kinematic assessment maps how joints and muscles respond to the body's forces. It detects asymmetries, compensations, weaknesses and movement dysfunctions of the body.

Precise diagnosis before treatment. It is not guesswork. It is science.

Movements, not just exercises. Your body was not made for mechanical repetition of exercises. It was made to go up and down stairs. It was not made exclusively for machines. It was made for real, everyday movements.

"Exercise is mechanical repetition. Movement is integrated function. And only movement gives you your life back."

Active longevity: the true goal

Andrea asks every new patient one question: "What do you want to be doing 10, 20, 30 years from now?"

Traveling without depending on anyone. Playing with the grandchildren. Practicing sports. Living without pain.

That is active longevity. It is not living longer. It is living better, with autonomy.

"Pain and limitation are not 'normal' parts of aging. They are consequences of years and can be reversed."

Instituto Trata exists for this: to give movement back so you can live better, without pain and with health.

When entrepreneurship stopped being lonely 

When she joined BNI, Andrea discovered something she did not expect: a network of friends who became partners. Qualified referrals who trust each other's work.

It was BNI that brought what she did not know she needed: a community of entrepreneurs who understand that businesses grow with trust, referrals and mutual support.

"Active longevity is not built alone. It is built in network."

For those who want to move better

Instituto Trata is for you who:

  • Suffers chronic pain and is tired of medications that don't solve it.
  • Wants to age with autonomy, not with limitations.
  • Practices sports and wants performance without injuries.
  • Has already had an injury and wants real rehabilitation.
  • Wants intelligent prevention.

From athletes to seniors. From executives to those seeking quality of life. Everyone wants to move with freedom of body movement.

The decision that changes your future

Now, the region has access to the new era of physiotherapy. Professionals who don't just relieve symptoms — they eliminate causes. A network of trust that understands that your health deserves the best.

The question is not whether you need it. It is: how long are you going to wait?

Every day of your life is worth gold; ignored pain, silent compensations... is one less day of free movement in your future.

Instituto Trata is ready. Andrea and her team are ready.

All that's left is for you to decide that your next years are going to be different ... different years and enjoying moments of leisure with more lightness.
